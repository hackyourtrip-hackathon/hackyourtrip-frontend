New York

New York is a waterfront metropolis of dazzling lights, high-kicking Broadway musicals, soaring bridges, vast parks, premier art museums, and award-winning cuisine and cocktails—all set against one of the world’s most famous skylines. Feast on pastrami sandwiches at Katz’s Delicatessen, followed by a creamy wedge of cheesecake at Junior’s. Trace the history of American art through the city’s museums, and come night-time, sip cocktails under the starry sky at one of the city’s hotel rooftop bars.
